Privacy
Policy

At DrainShield, we believe that security and privacy are inseparable. Our application is built on a foundation of zero-knowledge and non-custodial principles.

1. No Private Data Collection

DrainShield never asks for, stores, or transmits your private keys or seed phrases. All transaction signing happens locally within your own wallet application through WalletConnect.

2. Blockchain Data

To provide security analysis, we fetch public blockchain data via Moralis APIs. This data includes token approvals and contract interaction history. This information is publicly available on the blockchain and is not linked to your real-world identity by DrainShield.

3. Direct Interaction

All security revocations and "Panic Mode" actions are initiated by you. DrainShield acts as a tool to facilitate these actions but never takes control of your funds.

4. Open Source Commitment

Our code is open-source. For those who wish to verify our claims, we invite you to audit our repository on GitHub. Transparency is our greatest security feature.
