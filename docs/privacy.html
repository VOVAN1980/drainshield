Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Do Not Collect

DrainShield is built on principles of maximum privacy. We do NOT collect or store:

  • Private keys or Seed Phrases
  • Personal Identity Information (Name, Email, IP)
  • Financial account credentials

2. Wallet and Blockchain Data

We process public wallet addresses to scan token approvals through third-party indexing services (Moralis). This data is fetched in real-time and is not stored on centralized servers by IBITI Labs.

3. Local Storage

Configurations, such as your language preferences and linked wallet labels, are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to our infrastructure.

4. Contact

For questions regarding your privacy, contact: info@ibiticoin.com
